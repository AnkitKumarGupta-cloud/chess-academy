Our Story

Shaping Champions,
One Move at a Time

Founded in 2009 by Ankit Kumar Gupta — a FIDE-certified chess coach — Gupta Chess Classes was born from a single conviction: every mind, at any age, can be trained to think sharper, deeper, and further through chess.

Having competed in 120+ national and international tournaments and representing India at Asian Chess Championships, Ankit channelled decades of competitive experience into a coaching methodology blending classical theory with modern engine-assisted analysis.

"Chess is not merely a game — it is a mirror to the mind. We don't just teach moves; we build thinkers, problem-solvers, and champions."

— Ankit Kumar Gupta, Founder & Head Coach

Programs & Offerings

Our Training Programs

Structured learning paths designed for every skill level — from the very first move to grandmaster ambitions.

♙

Beginner Batches

For children (age 5+) and first-time adult learners. Build a strong foundation with piece movements, tactics, and game fundamentals in a nurturing environment.

  • Rules & piece movements
  • Basic opening principles
  • Tactical patterns & puzzles
  • Endgame fundamentals
♗

Advanced Training

For rated or experienced players seeking serious improvement. Deep dive into positional play, custom opening repertoires, and complex tactical patterns.

  • Personalized game analysis
  • Opening repertoire building
  • Advanced endgame mastery
  • Engine-assisted study sessions
♛

Tournament Prep

Intensive preparation for state, national & FIDE-rated tournaments. Simulated match conditions, psychological coaching, and time management training.

  • Simulated match play
  • Psychological preparation
  • Clock management drills
  • Opponent research & prep
♞

Online Classes

Learn from anywhere in India. Live interactive sessions on Zoom with shared Lichess Study boards, matching the depth of our in-person classes.

  • Live interactive sessions
  • Shared Lichess analysis boards
  • Recorded class archive
  • Structured homework system
♜

One-on-One Coaching

Hyper-personalized 1:1 sessions directly with Ankit Kumar Gupta or senior coaches. Rapid improvement, laser-focused on your specific weaknesses and goals.

  • Custom study plan & roadmap
  • Detailed weakness identification
  • Unlimited Q&A & support
  • Monthly milestone reports
♟

School Programs

Curriculum-integrated chess programs for schools. We partner with institutions to make chess a structured co-curricular subject with measurable outcomes.

  • Curriculum-aligned lessons
  • Inter-school tournaments
  • Teacher training workshops
  • Digital progress tracking
